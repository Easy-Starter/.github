# Governance

[نسخه فارسی](./fa/GOVERNANCE.fa.md)

Easy Starter uses a maintainer-led governance model.

## Roles

- **Contributors** propose issues, documentation, code, and reviews.
- **Maintainers** review changes, manage releases, enforce project policies, and own one or more repositories.
- **Organization owner** sets ecosystem direction, appoints maintainers, resolves cross-repository conflicts, and has final responsibility for security and legal decisions.

## Decisions

Routine repository decisions are made by the responsible maintainers through review. Significant architecture, policy, naming, compatibility, or cross-repository decisions require a written proposal or ADR and reasonable review time. The organization owner resolves deadlocks.

## Releases and deprecation

Maintainers use semantic versioning where applicable, document breaking changes, provide migration guidance, and clearly mark deprecated or archived repositories.

## Conflicts of interest

Maintainers should disclose material conflicts and avoid being the sole approver of changes that directly benefit an undisclosed commercial interest.
