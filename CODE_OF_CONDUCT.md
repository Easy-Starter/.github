# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Easy Starter is committed to a respectful, welcoming, and practical community. Contributors must communicate professionally, critique ideas rather than people, respect different levels of experience, and protect private information.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, intimidation, sexualized conduct, threats, doxxing, deliberate disruption, and publishing another person's private information without permission.

## Scope

This policy applies in repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, community spaces, and public representation of the project.

## Reporting and enforcement

Do not debate serious conduct reports publicly. Contact organization maintainers privately through the organization profile. Maintainers may edit or remove content, restrict participation, temporarily suspend access, or permanently ban participants based on severity and pattern.

Reports will be handled as confidentially and fairly as practical. Retaliation against a reporter is prohibited.
